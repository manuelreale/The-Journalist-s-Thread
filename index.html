THE JOURNALIST'S THREAD




The Journalist's Thread is a machine learning based tool that support decision making during the newsgathering process. The service was developed in collaboration with journalists and embodies journalistic values, turning the average journalist into a Data Journalist without having to worry about algorythmic biases.
